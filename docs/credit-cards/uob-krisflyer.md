# UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card

## Overview

The UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card is the ultimate travel companion for frequent flyers and aviation enthusiasts who want to accumulate Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer miles with every dollar they spend. This premium co-branded card offers one of the most generous mile accrual rates in Singapore at 1.5 KrisFlyer miles per S$1 spent on general purchases, and up to 3 miles per S$1 when you spend on Singapore Airlines and SilkAir bookings.

As Singapore's flagship carrier, Singapore Airlines' KrisFlyer program is renowned for its premium redemption options including access to luxurious Suites and First Class cabins, extensive partner airline network through Star Alliance, and the ability to book award flights with minimal surcharges. The UOB KrisFlyer card seamlessly integrates with your KrisFlyer account, with miles automatically credited every month, bringing you closer to your next award flight with every swipe.

This card is ideal for business travelers, expatriates who travel frequently to their home countries, and anyone who dreams of experiencing the world's best airline in premium cabins. With complimentary travel insurance and exclusive lounge access benefits, the UOB KrisFlyer card delivers comprehensive value beyond just miles earning.

## Key Benefits

- **1.5 KrisFlyer miles per S$1 spent** - Industry-leading mile accrual rate on all local and foreign spending, helping you earn award flights faster than standard rewards cards
- **3 KrisFlyer miles per S$1 on Singapore Airlines** - Triple miles when you book flights and other services directly with Singapore Airlines and SilkAir
- **Automatic mile crediting** - Miles earned from card spending are automatically transferred to your KrisFlyer account monthly without any manual conversion needed
- **Complimentary priority pass** - Receive 4 complimentary airport lounge visits per year through Priority Pass membership at over 1,300 lounges worldwide
- **Comprehensive travel insurance** - Enjoy coverage up to S$750,000 for overseas travel, including trip cancellation, lost baggage, and medical emergencies
- **Bonus miles on sign-up** - New cardholders receive up to 10,000 bonus miles when minimum spending requirements are met within the first 3 months

## Eligibility

The UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card has premium eligibility criteria reflecting its status as a travel card:

- **Minimum Annual Income**: S$60,000 per year for Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents; S$80,000 per year for foreigners
- **Citizenship**: Open to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and Employment Pass holders
- **Age**: Applicants must be at least 21 years old
- **KrisFlyer Membership**: You must have an existing Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer account; if you don't have one, you'll need to register (free) before or during the card application
- **Credit Standing**: Good credit history with no adverse records, defaults, or bankruptcies in the past 5 years
- **Employment**: Must be employed with a stable income source or self-employed with verifiable income documentation

## How to Apply

Here's how to apply for the UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card:

1. **Ensure you have a KrisFlyer account** - Visit singaporeair.com to create your free KrisFlyer membership if you don't already have one; note your KrisFlyer number as you'll need it during application
2. **Visit UOB's credit card portal** - Navigate to the UOB KrisFlyer Credit Card page on uob.com.sg and click the "Apply Now" button to access the online application form
3. **Complete the application form** - Enter your personal particulars (name, NRIC/passport, date of birth), contact information, residential address, employment details, and most importantly, your KrisFlyer membership number for automatic mile crediting
4. **Provide income and financial details** - Declare your gross annual income, existing credit facilities, monthly loan repayments, and other financial commitments; this information determines your credit limit eligibility
5. **Upload supporting documents** - Submit digital copies of your NRIC (front and back), latest computerized payslip, and latest Income Tax Notice of Assessment (NOA) or CPF contribution statement
6. **Wait for approval and card delivery** - UOB will process your application within 5-7 business days; you'll receive an SMS and email notification once approved; the card will be delivered to your mailing address via registered mail within 10-14 working days; activate it immediately upon receipt to start earning miles
