# DBS Live Fresh Credit Card

## Overview

The DBS Live Fresh Credit Card is Singapore's first card designed exclusively for young working adults aged 21 to 29. This no-fee card offers a refreshing approach to credit cards with rotating cashback categories that change monthly, allowing young professionals to maximize rewards based on their lifestyle needs. Whether you're into fitness, dining, or travel, the card adapts to reward what matters most to you each month.

With an attractive 5% cashback rate on selected categories and no annual fee ever, this card removes the barriers typically associated with premium credit cards. The card also features contactless payment capabilities and seamless integration with the DBS PayLah! app for convenient mobile payments and real-time transaction tracking.

DBS Live Fresh is perfect for young Singaporeans who are building their credit history while enjoying meaningful rewards without the burden of annual fees. The card grows with you, offering automatic upgrades to premium DBS cards once you exceed the age limit.

## Key Benefits

- **5% cashback on selected categories** - Earn high cashback rates on rotating categories that change monthly (e.g., fitness, dining, entertainment, online shopping, transport)
- **1% cashback on all other spending** - Even purchases outside featured categories earn you returns
- **No annual fee forever** - Enjoy premium rewards without paying a single dollar in annual fees, even after the first year
- **Exclusive for ages 21-29** - Specially designed for young adults with relevant lifestyle benefits and partnerships
- **Instant notifications** - Get real-time alerts on your phone for every transaction to track spending and detect fraud immediately
- **Automatic upgrade path** - Seamlessly transition to DBS Altitude or other premium cards when you outgrow the age criteria

## Eligibility

To qualify for the DBS Live Fresh Credit Card, applicants must meet these criteria:

- **Minimum Annual Income**: S$20,000 per year (lower than most cards to accommodate fresh graduates and early-career professionals)
- **Citizenship**: Open to Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents only
- **Age**: Must be between 21 to 29 years old (strictly enforced - card will be converted after 30th birthday)
- **Employment Status**: Must be employed with verifiable income; fresh graduates with job offers accepted
- **Credit Record**: Clean credit history preferred; first-time credit card applicants welcome

## How to Apply

Getting your DBS Live Fresh Card is quick and easy:

1. **Check your eligibility** - Confirm you meet the age requirement (21-29 years) and minimum income of S$20,000 per year
2. **Apply online via DBS website** - Navigate to the DBS Live Fresh product page and click "Apply Now"; the entire process can be completed digitally without visiting a branch
3. **Fill in the application form** - Provide your NRIC number, contact details, employment information, and monthly income; use your Singpass for instant income verification via MyInfo
4. **Submit digital documents** - Upload photos of your NRIC (front and back) and latest payslip or CPF contribution statement if Singpass verification is not used
5. **Receive instant preliminary approval** - Most applicants get an instant decision; if further review is needed, expect feedback within 2-3 business days via email or SMS
6. **Activate your card upon delivery** - Your new card arrives within 5-7 working days; activate it via the DBS app, SMS, or phone hotline and start using it immediately

| Late Payment Fee | S$100 per month |
| Interest on Purchases | 26.0% p.a. |
| Interest on Cash Advances | 26.0% p.a. |
| Cash Advance Fee | 6% of advance amount (minimum S$15) |
| Foreign Currency Transaction Fee | 3.5% of transaction amount |
| Card Replacement Fee | S$21.40 per card |
| Overlimit Fee | S$40 per occurrence |
| Returned Cheque/GIRO Fee | S$40 per returned payment |
