# Credit Cards Overview

MoneyHero offers five credit cards in Singapore. Here is a summary of each card to help you find the best match.

## Available Credit Cards

- **HSBC Revolution Card** — Best for travel and dining rewards. Earn up to 10x points on travel bookings and 5x points on dining. Points convert to miles with 12+ airline partners including Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer and Asia Miles. Annual fee waived with S$24,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$50,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$70,000 for foreigners.

- **Citi Cashback Plus Card** — Best for everyday cashback. Earn up to 8% cashback on groceries and petrol at selected merchants, and 6% on dining. Cashback is credited automatically with no minimum redemption. Annual fee waived with S$10,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$42,000 for foreigners.

- **DBS Live Fresh Card** — Best for online shopping and entertainment. Earn 5% cashback on online shopping (Shopee, Lazada, Amazon) and streaming services (Netflix, Spotify). A separate 5% cashback tier applies to contactless payments. Annual fee waived with S$25,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$45,000 for foreigners.

- **OCBC 365 Card** — Best for dining and recurring bills. Earn 6% cashback at restaurants and food delivery, 3% on groceries and utilities, and 0.3% on all other purchases. Minimum monthly spend of S$800 required to unlock cashback tiers. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$45,000 for foreigners.

- **UOB KrisFlyer Card** — Best for frequent flyers on Singapore Airlines. Earn KrisFlyer miles directly with no points conversion required. Earn 3 miles per S$1 on SIA Group purchases and 1.2 miles per S$1 on everyday spend. Complimentary travel insurance and two lounge visits per year. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$40,000 for foreigners.

## How to Choose

- Want travel miles? Consider the HSBC Revolution Card or UOB KrisFlyer Card.
- Want cashback on daily spending? Consider the Citi Cashback Plus Card or OCBC 365 Card.
- Shop online frequently? Consider the DBS Live Fresh Card.
- Fly Singapore Airlines often? The UOB KrisFlyer Card credits miles instantly.
- Dine out regularly? The OCBC 365 Card offers the highest dining cashback rate at 6%.

## Eligibility

All five cards are available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and valid Employment Pass holders aged 21 and above with a clean credit history. Minimum income requirements vary by card and residency status as listed above.
