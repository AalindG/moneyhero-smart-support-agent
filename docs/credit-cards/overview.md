# Credit Cards Overview

MoneyHero offers five credit cards in Hong Kong. Below is a summary of each card to help you find the best match.

## Available Credit Cards

| Card | Best For | Key Benefit |
|------|----------|-------------|
| HSBC Revolution | Travel & dining rewards | Up to 10x points on travel, 5x on dining |
| Citi Cashback Plus | Everyday cashback | Up to 8% cashback on groceries, petrol, dining |
| DBS Live Fresh | Online shopping & entertainment | 5% cashback on online spend and Spotify/Netflix |
| OCBC 365 | Recurring bills & dining | Up to 6% cashback on dining, 3% on utilities |
| UOB KrisFlyer | Frequent flyers | Earn KrisFlyer miles directly, no conversion needed |

## Card Summaries

### HSBC Revolution
A versatile rewards card earning up to 10x points on travel bookings and 5x points on dining. Points convert to miles with 12+ airline partners including Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer and Asia Miles. Annual fee waived with S$24,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$50,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$70,000 for foreigners.

### Citi Cashback Plus
A cashback card earning up to 8% on groceries and petrol at selected merchants and 6% on dining. Cashback is credited automatically with no minimum redemption. Annual fee waived with S$10,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs.

### DBS Live Fresh
An online-first cashback card earning 5% on online shopping (Shopee, Lazada, Amazon) and streaming services (Netflix, Spotify). A separate 5% cashback tier applies to contactless payments. Annual fee waived with S$25,000 annual spend. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs.

### OCBC 365
A dining and bills card earning 6% cashback at restaurants and food delivery, 3% on groceries and utilities, and 0.3% on all other purchases. Minimum monthly spend of S$800 required to unlock cashback tiers. Minimum income: S$30,000.

### UOB KrisFlyer
A miles card that earns KrisFlyer miles directly — no points conversion required. Earn 3 miles per S$1 on SIA Group purchases and 1.2 miles per S$1 on everyday spend. Complimentary travel insurance and two lounge visits per year. Minimum income: S$30,000 for Singaporeans/PRs, S$40,000 for foreigners.

## How to Choose

- **Want travel miles?** → HSBC Revolution or UOB KrisFlyer
- **Want cashback on daily spending?** → Citi Cashback Plus or OCBC 365
- **Shop online frequently?** → DBS Live Fresh
- **Fly Singapore Airlines often?** → UOB KrisFlyer (miles credited instantly)
- **Dine out regularly?** → OCBC 365 (highest dining cashback rate)

## Eligibility Summary

All five cards are available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and valid Employment Pass holders aged 21 and above with a clean credit history.
