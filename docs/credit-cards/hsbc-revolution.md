# HSBC Revolution Credit Card

## Overview

The HSBC Revolution Credit Card is a versatile rewards card that empowers you to earn points on everything you spend while giving you the flexibility to choose how you redeem them. With an innovative tiered rewards structure, cardholders earn up to 10x points on travel bookings, 5x points on dining, and 2x points on all other purchases. This makes it one of the most rewarding cards for well-rounded spenders who don't want to limit themselves to a single category.

What sets the HSBC Revolution apart is its comprehensive redemption ecosystem. Convert your points to air miles with over 12 airline partners including Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, Asia Miles, and British Airways Avios, or redeem for cashback, shopping vouchers, hotel stays, or even statement credits. The card also features premium travel benefits including airport lounge access and travel insurance coverage up to S$1,000,000.

Designed for middle to high-income earners, the HSBC Revolution strikes the perfect balance between earning power and redemption flexibility, making it ideal for those who want maximum value from their credit card spending without being locked into a single rewards program.

## Key Benefits

- **10x Rewards Points on travel** - Earn accelerated points when you book flights, hotels, and travel packages through HSBC's travel portal or directly with airlines and hotels
- **5x Rewards Points on dining** - Get 5x points at restaurants, cafes, bars, and food delivery services islandwide
- **2x Rewards Points on everything else** - All other spending earns double points, from groceries to online shopping to utilities
- **Flexible redemption options** - Convert points to miles with 12+ airline programs, redeem for cash rebates, shopping vouchers, or use for travel bookings
- **Annual fee waived with spending** - No annual fee if you spend S$24,000 in the previous year (S$2,000 per month average)
- **Complimentary airport lounge access** - Enjoy 6 complimentary lounge visits per year at Changi Airport and overseas airports via Priority Pass

## Eligibility

The HSBC Revolution Credit Card has the following eligibility requirements:

- **Minimum Annual Income**: S$50,000 per year for Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents; S$70,000 per year for foreigners
- **Citizenship**: Available to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and valid Employment Pass holders
- **Age**: Applicant must be at least 21 years old with no upper age limit
- **HSBC Relationship**: No existing HSBC relationship required; new-to-bank customers welcome
- **Credit History**: Good credit standing required; applicants with recent defaults or bankruptcies will be declined
- **Employment**: Must have stable employment or self-employment with verifiable income documentation

## How to Apply

Follow these steps to apply for your HSBC Revolution Credit Card:

1. **Start your online application** - Visit the HSBC Singapore website and locate the Revolution Credit Card product page; click "Apply Now" to begin the digital application process
2. **Complete the application form** - Provide comprehensive personal details including full name, NRIC/passport number, residential address, employment details, and monthly income; ensure all information matches your official documents
3. **Declare financial information** - Disclose your gross annual income, existing credit facilities with other banks, and monthly financial commitments to help HSBC assess your credit limit
4. **Upload required documents** - Submit clear digital copies of your NRIC (or passport and work permit for foreigners), latest payslip or income tax assessment, and proof of residential address dated within the last 3 months
5. **Await application review** - HSBC will process your application within 3-7 business days; you'll receive updates via email and SMS; some applications may require additional verification or documentation
6. **Receive and activate your card** - Upon approval, your HSBC Revolution card will be delivered via courier to your registered address within 7-10 working days; activate it by calling the HSBC hotline or through the HSBC Singapore app
