# Citi Cashback Plus Credit Card

## Overview

The Citi Cashback Plus Credit Card is designed for savvy shoppers who want to maximize rewards on their everyday spending. With industry-leading cashback rates on online shopping and groceries, this card is ideal for Singapore residents who frequently shop on e-commerce platforms like Lazada, Shopee, and Amazon, as well as those who do regular grocery shopping at FairPrice, Cold Storage, and Sheng Siong.

This premium cashback card offers up to 8% cashback on online purchases, 5% on groceries, and 2% on all other spending, making it one of the most rewarding cards for digital-first consumers. The card comes with complimentary travel insurance and purchase protection for added peace of mind.

Whether you're stocking up on household essentials or treating yourself to the latest gadgets online, the Citi Cashback Plus ensures you get substantial returns on every dollar spent.

## Key Benefits

- **8% cashback on online shopping** - Earn the highest cashback rate in Singapore on all online purchases including e-commerce platforms, food delivery, and ride-hailing apps
- **5% cashback on groceries** - Get rewarded every time you shop at major supermarkets and grocery stores islandwide
- **2% cashback on everything else** - Even your regular spending earns you cashback, from dining to petrol to utility bills
- **No minimum spend requirement** - Start earning cashback from your first dollar spent with no complicated thresholds to meet
- **Complimentary travel insurance** - Enjoy coverage up to S$500,000 when you charge your travel expenses to the card
- **First year annual fee waived** - Try the card risk-free with no annual fee for the first 12 months

## Eligibility

To apply for the Citi Cashback Plus Credit Card, you must meet the following requirements:

- **Minimum Annual Income**: S$30,000 per year for Singapore Citizens and Permanent Residents; S$45,000 per year for foreigners
- **Citizenship**: Open to Singapore Citizens, Permanent Residents, and Employment Pass holders
- **Age**: Minimum 21 years old
- **Credit History**: Good credit standing with no recent bankruptcies or defaults
- **Employment**: Must be employed or self-employed with verifiable income

## How to Apply

Follow these simple steps to apply for your Citi Cashback Plus Credit Card:

1. **Visit the Citi website or MoneyHero platform** - Access the online application form through the official Citi bank website or through comparison platforms like MoneyHero for potential sign-up bonuses
2. **Prepare required documents** - Have your NRIC (for Singaporeans/PRs) or passport and employment pass (for foreigners), latest payslips or income tax notice, and proof of address ready
3. **Complete the online application form** - Fill in your personal details, employment information, and financial particulars accurately; the form takes approximately 10-15 minutes to complete
4. **Submit supporting documents** - Upload clear copies of your NRIC, latest payslip, and CPF contribution statement for income verification
5. **Wait for approval notification** - Citi will review your application within 3-5 business days and notify you via SMS and email
6. **Receive your card** - Once approved, your new credit card will be delivered to your registered address via registered mail within 7-14 working days
